Australian Landscapes Unveiled

Explore the vast and varied terrain of Australia, from rugged coastlines to sprawling deserts.

Common Features

  1. Vast stretches of arid outback dominate the interior.
  2. Dramatic coastal cliffs line the southern shores.
  3. Ancient rock formations rise from the earth.
  4. Unique ecosystems thrive in isolation.

Iconic Landscapes

  • The red sands of the Simpson Desert.
  • The towering limestone stacks of the Twelve Apostles.
  • The vibrant coral reefs of the Great Barrier Reef.
  • The lush rainforests of Queensland.

Defining Traits

Diversity
Australia boasts deserts, beaches, and mountains in one continent.
Scale
Expansive landscapes stretch across millions of square kilometers.
Color
From ochre reds to turquoise waters, the palette is vivid.
Timelessness
Geological wonders date back billions of years.
